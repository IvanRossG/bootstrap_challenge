Hello World

I'd love to introduce myself into the dev community 🖤. I've been around reading posts for a few months and I believe it's time to start sharing my knowledge and experience.

Who I am?

First of all, I'm Cale. A 21-year-old boy, currently studying Computer Engineering Technology in Canada🍁, I'm originally from Venezuela though.

Programming has changed my life. Since a I was a child I started to develop curiosity about computers and "hacking" 💻. I was so sure what I wanted to be that I started to study by myself since and I'm pretty proud of my decision.

Currently, I'm learning and improving my HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React skills every day. I want to become a front-end developer 👨🏻‍💻, aspiring to Full Stack Developer though.

Why am I starting to write? ✏📚

I've learned about experienced developers, the best way to learn is teaching, sharing and helping others. I've learned so much reading posts from developers I don't even know and I believe it's my turn to help others developers ✨.

Gratefulness 🤍

Thank you so much to all developers out there sharing their journey and knowledge. I truly believe I'm taking a good decision joining this loved community.
